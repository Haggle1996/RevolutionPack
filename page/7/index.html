REVOLUTION|2 - Beta 2.1.3.0 Update!

Hello, everyone! Welcome to REVOLUTION|2 Beta 2.1.3.0 – the ‘Crusher’ Update! After a solid week of playtesting, we’ve gotten a lot of good feedback, and this update is chock full o’ quality of life updates. In particular, we’ve switched from a blacklist approach to physics, to a whitelist approach. What this means is fewer blocks will fall unexpectedly. We’ve also increased the default strength of most blocks. In exchange, we’ve turned on slide physics. »
