The 'It works better!' Update, REVOLUTION|3 3.6.1!

Mar 19, 2017 #REVOLUTION|3

Hi all!

Small update to address the TPS and FPS issues, as well as make oceans and rivers melt!

Pack Changes

  • Players upgrading maps should receive a message about missing blocks. This is expected and okay to proceed.
  • Ice should now immediately melt, instead of never melting – seasons should function much better now!
  • Lowered the MCA villager cap to 12
  • Removed Zombie Awareness and Special AI until I can find a more TPS-friendly means of killing you. This makes the pack slightly easier.

Mods Removed

  • Zombie Awareness
  • Special AI
